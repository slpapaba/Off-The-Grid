Basic Necessities For Living Off The Grid

  • Land (a good location)
  • Shelter (build or buy before moving)
  • Water (a good clean potable water source)
  • Food (grow your own food)
  • Power Generation System (generate electricity yourself)
  • Power Storage System (where it gets expensive)
  • Waste Disposal & Septic System (build a proper septic system)
  • The Mindset & Will to Live Off The Grid (the most important thing)
